About Me

I am an astrophysics PhD student at the Janusz Gil Institute of Astronomy, University of Zielona Góra, Poland. My PhD focuses on the scattering effects of the interstellar medium on radio pulsars. Outside of astrophysics, I also explore the philosophy of science, which I consider the backbone of the scientific method.
